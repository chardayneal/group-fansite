Guacamole

Guacamole is a dip or spread made from avocados, with origins in Mexico. It's usually prepared by mashing avocados with lime juice, then adding salt and cilantro for flavor. Common additional ingredients include tomatoes and onions.

Ingredients

  • 3 avocados - peeled, pitted, and mashed
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 roma (plum) tomatoes, diced
  • Â½ cup diced onion
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 pinch ground cayenne pepper (Optional)

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Mash avocados, lime juice, and salt together in a medium bowl; mix in tomatoes, onion, cilantro, and garlic.
  3. Stir in cayenne pepper. Serve immediately, or cover and refrigerate for 1 hour for improved flavor.
